Research

My work focuses on quantum-state-resolved molecular beam scattering, alignment and orientation-controlled chemical reactivity, and phonon-mediated energy transfer in molecule–surface interactions.

I completed my PhD at TIFR Hyderabad on "Dissociation dynamics of CO₂ on Cu(110) and developing techniques for state-selected molecule-surface scattering experiments".

Research Interests

  • Quantum-state-resolved molecular scattering
  • Gas–surface reaction dynamics
  • Phonon-mediated dissipation and energy transfer
  • Molecular alignment/orientation control
  • High-resolution molecular laser spectroscopy
  • Helium spin-echo interferometry
