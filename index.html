About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Swansea University, focusing on alignment- and orientation-controlled reactive scattering processes in gas–surface interactions, with emphasis on rotationally controlled H₂ dynamics, HELICOPTER and CARTWHEEL states, and phonon-mediated surface energy transfer.

Research Overview

My research spans molecular beam scattering, quantum state preparation, surface reaction dynamics, and high-resolution laser spectroscopy. I investigate how molecular orientation, alignment, and vibrational state influence reactivity on metal surfaces, using advanced interferometric and scattering methods.
