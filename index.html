About Me

Postdoctoral Researcher in surface dynamics, molecular scattering, and gas–surface reaction mechanisms.

Research Overview

My research is divided into CO₂ dissociation on metal surfaces and development of advanced diagnostic tools.

Key Research Impact

  • Measured CO₂ dissociation probability down to 10⁻⁵ range
  • Identified energy-dependent reaction pathways
  • Developed MHz-level laser stabilization systems
  • Built compact diagnostic instruments for surface science
