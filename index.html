About Me

Postdoctoral Researcher working on stereodynamics of molecule–surface interactions, quantum-state resolved scattering, and gas–surface reaction dynamics.

Research Overview

My research is divided into two areas: CO₂ dissociation on metal surfaces and development of advanced diagnostic tools.

Does CO₂ Dissociate on Low-Index Copper Surfaces?

Study of CO₂ dissociation on Cu surfaces under UHV conditions. Focus on incident energy, molecular orientation, and vibrational effects. Important for heterogeneous catalysis and CO₂ reduction.
